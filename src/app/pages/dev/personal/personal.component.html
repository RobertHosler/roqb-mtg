The Objective Personality System (OPS) is a personality typology system developed by Dave and Shannon Powers based on the theories of Carl Jung. While the more well known Myers Briggs Type Indicator has 16 types, their system adds additional layers of complexity to create 512 unique types. This app generates a type description and visual aid for each of the 512 types in the system by combining various definitions for each of the coins.

This app is hosted on the subjectivepersonality domain, which was created to host a wordpress blog that I started with another member of the community in an attempt to better organize and distribute some of the information pertaining to the system to those interested.

The app was developed using Angular and first launched in July 2020. As of July 2021 it has served over 2000 unique visitors according to Google Analytics data. It is hosted by Heroku.

Magic the Gathering is a card game I've been playing off and on since I was in elementary school. This application allows you to draft cards from a set selection of cards in multiple 2 player formats with another user.

  • Winston - choose from 3 face down piles
  • Pancake - choose from a pack of 11
  • Burn Four - choose 1 and remove 4 from a pack of 15
  • Glimpse - choose 1 and remove 2 from a pack of 15
  • Grid - choose a row or column from a 9 by 9 grid of cards
  • Winchester - choose from one of 4 face up piles

This app was developed using PHP and Javascript and hosted via Heroku.
