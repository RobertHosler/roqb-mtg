I am an Artist

My relationship with art began almost by accident. In high school I wanted to take a computer design course, but it ended up being filled with other students who received priority. Rather than take a study hall I decided to try a drawing and painting course. This course ended up challenging us each week to create a unique sketch based on a theme each week. This is something that really captured my imagination and something I really miss about my high school art classes. When I went to college I wanted to keep taking art classes and asked if a double major was even possible since there would be no overlapping coursework. I made it happen. Here I'll present some of my favorite creations.

High School Works

MTG Alters and Proxies

Magic the Gathering is a card game I've been playing for many years. Sometimes people paint on the cards to alter them in some way. Either creating new art or extending the existing art in some way. I've dabbled in this in the past and even created proxy cards which are meant to represent a real card without actually having the card.
