High School Works

Magic the Gathering is a card game I've been playing for many years. Sometimes people paint on the cards to alter them in some way, either creating new art or extending the existing art.

I've dabbled in this in the past and created "proxy cards" which can stand in for a real card without actually having the card to play the game. This can be useful for particularly expensive cards as a single card can sometimes cost over a hundred dollars, but is also just a fun way to explore different kinds of art within the game.
