Throughout my life I've occasionally dabbled in the art of writing. Below are some of the projects I've worked on.

The Objective Personality System is a personality system building on the works of Carl Jung and the MBTI. This system is highly complex and contains 512 unique personality types. The Subjective Personality site was originally created as a site where I could begin to break down and explain some of the concepts from this system in written form. The creators of the system largely communicate their ideas through video and I felt the lack of written material on the system needed to be addresseed.
